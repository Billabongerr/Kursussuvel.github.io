Psyche of the BEST: Mind your own business!

2018

Our 2018 course was on the theme of Business Psychology. Participants visited popular Estonian start-ups and big companies to see their business strategies. Learned how psychology and business are connected and how they could use it in their lives and workplaces. Also, we got a better understanding of web marketing and IT-companies.

Oh no you WOODn't!

2017

Our 2017 course was on the theme of WOOD. Learning about typical and mechanical qualities of wood and how to process wood with CAD and CNC-technology. At the end of the course each participant had made their own wood object which was made during the practical part. Also during company visits participants saw how different objects are made out of wood.
